Balsamic Bruschetta

Description

Balsamic Bruschetta is a delightful Italian appetizer featuring toasted bread topped with a mixture of fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and balsamic vinegar. It's a simple yet flavorful dish that is perfect for entertaining or as a snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 French baguette, sliced
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C). Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and lightly brush with olive oil.
  2. Toast in the oven for about 5-7 minutes, until golden and crispy.
  3. In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, basil, garlic, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Stir well to combine.
  4. Top each toasted baguette slice with the tomato mixture.
  5. Garnish with Parmesan cheese if desired and serve immediately.
