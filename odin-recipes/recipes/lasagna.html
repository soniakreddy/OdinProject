Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted cheese. This hearty meal is perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

With a blend of flavorful tomato sauce, ground beef, and perfectly baked layers of pasta, this lasagna recipe is both comforting and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (24 oz) jar of pasta sauce
  • 1 (15 oz) container of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, then drain.
  3. In a large pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the onions and garlic, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in the pasta sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with the egg.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used.
  7. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Bake for 45 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
